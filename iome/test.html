Identity for all your web3 and web2 applications
Personalized and decentralized identity management for the sovereign individual
IOMe is a user-owned decentralized identity and authentication solution that lets users interoperate between web2 and web3 networks. It extends MOI protocol's identity infrastructure with zero-knowledge technology to provide easy, and secure digital interactions.
A Digital You to manage your digital life
Manage your identity
We categorized the digital information into several dimensions to let you have the complete control over your digital information in seamless and privacy preserved manner.
Manage your apps
We ensure user give consent and know about every piece of information that is being shared with applications, and user has power to revoke the access anytime, while every interaction is powered by advanced cryptography.
Managing your real-life interactions
We allow user to create multiple digital avatars based on behaviour to enable context-specific interactions by sharing only the necessary identity information without compromising other personal information.
Easily connect your digital and physical personas
Simple and intuitive access
Access any digital application using a simple username and password. IOMe never stores your credentials and utilizes ZK-based verification and authentication.
Secure data management
Only you have full access to your own data. IOMe uses advanced public key cryptography to enable tamper-proof data storage with reduced risk.
Choose what you share
Based on role behaviour of the user with that digital application, user has control over choosing what all information to be shared.
Portable and private
IOMe implements DIDs and VCs to deliver portable and privacy-preserving data access, yet publicly verifiable.
Context-aware
IOMe allows users to create behavior-based roles based on the context of the digital interactions and exchanging only necessary information.
Seamless interoperability
IOMe enables seamless interoperation for users by building participant native connectivity between blockchains, web2, and web3 networks.
A quick sneak-peek at the gears that run the machine
IOMe is the new form of identity, that weaves together technologies to achieve key identity attributes such as self-ownership of data, censorship resistance and greater assurance of immutability.
DIDs and VCs
The bedrock of IOMe lies in the application of Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) on a blockchain and Verified Credentials (VCs) that are issued by reputable organizations and validated using cryptographic proof.
ZKP Authentication
IOMe employs non-interactive zero-knowledge technology, specifically zk-SNARK, to enable password-less authentication and global verification.
MOI ID
MOI ID's decentralized identity specification is based on natural, sustainable human behaviors to facilitate participant-centric digital interactions.
Interaction State Machine
ISM is a new computational model that introduces separate and independent dimension to the current model of the Turing machine to represent the possible behaviour of the participant in the network.
MOI Bit
Using MOI Bit, IOMe ensures regulatory compliance and provides risk-mitigated tamper-proof data storage through advanced public key cryptography.
